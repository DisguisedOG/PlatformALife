# Glossary

## Beneficiaries
Starter characters in the NZ Platformer project. Beneficiaries are the player’s initial archetype and determine early available Jobs.

## Jobs
A Job is the player’s chosen progression path, similar to classic RPG classes. Jobs unlock job-specific Skills and provide role identity in the game.

## Skills
Skills are the active abilities and passive upgrades tied to Jobs. Skills can be gained as Jobs level up and are the core growth mechanic for each Job.

## Ron Lockout
A persistent 30-day lockout that prevents the creation of the Ron entity more than once within the lockout window. The lockout is stored in the save data.

## Job Tier Progression
The progression system has three milestone levels:
- Level 10: Tier 1 unlock
- Level 30: Tier 2 unlock
- Level 70: Tier 3 unlock
